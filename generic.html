Internet Of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) pertains to the interconnection of various devices (beyond conventional computers and smartphones) with the internet, enabling them to transmit and receive data. These devices encompass a wide range, extending from commonplace items such as thermostats and refrigerators to industrial machinery like tractors and airplanes. The primary objective of IoT is to facilitate the exchange of information among devices, fostering collaboration to enhance efficiency, minimize human intervention, and elevate safety standards. Instances of IoT in action include smart residences, connected vehicles, and wearable fitness monitoring devices.
