Cyber Security

Cyber security is the proactive approach of safeguarding computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data against cyber threats, unauthorized access, and potential harm. It encompasses the deployment of advanced technologies, systematic processes, and comprehensive policies to fortify networks, devices, and data, preventing unauthorized entry, misuse, disclosure, disruption, alteration, or destruction. Cyber resilience is imperative for enterprises, institutions, and individuals alike, serving as a fundamental strategy to safeguard confidential information, uphold data privacy, and guarantee the steadfastness and accessibility of digital resources.
